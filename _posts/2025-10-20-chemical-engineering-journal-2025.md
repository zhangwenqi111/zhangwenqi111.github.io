---

本课题组刘晓蕾同学在李安婕副教授的指导下完成的题为 **"Bio-entrapment of Chlorella vulgaris mimicking granular sludge: Insights into aggregated microalgae growth and symbiotic bacteria under antibiotic stress"** 的研究论文，被化工领域顶级期刊 **《Chemical Engineering Journal》**（IF=15.1）接收发表。
